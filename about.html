ABOUT ME

Cloe Young is a fashion and documentary photographer based in New York City who focuses on documenting the lives of trans and gender non-conforming youth. Using her own life experiences to inform her photographs, she creates moments in between fantasy and realism in order to engage with the realities that her communities live through.
